About Me

Kristoph Lederer holds a BBA and MBA from Marshall University Brad Smith College of Business and is currently pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) at Georgetown University McDonough School of Business, where he has received a merit scholarship.

With expertise in SQL, Python, and data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI, Kristoph specializes in data transformation, ETL processes, automation, and predictive modeling. His experience includes leading data analytics projects for government and private sector clients, implementing numerous process automations, and deploying cloud-based solutions using AWS and Azure.

Kristoph has worked as a Lead Data Analyst, where he developed data engineering strategies, automated workflows, and built interactive dashboards for government contracts. His previous roles in executive search and business development involved data-driven research, ETL pipeline development, and CRM system optimization.

For those interested in data analytics, automation, and business intelligence, Kristoph continues to develop and implement solutions that drive efficiency and insights.
